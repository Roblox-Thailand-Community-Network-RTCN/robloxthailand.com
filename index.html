Who are we?

Roblox Thailand Community & Network (RTCN) is an organization dedicated to bringing Thai Roblox players together, supporting various initiatives, and organizing events to strengthen community connections. We are passionate about the Roblox platform and the Metaverse.

The organization focuses on developing and promoting communication within the Roblox Thailand community, with most activities and events managed through Facebook.
